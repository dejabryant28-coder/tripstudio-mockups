Insider Tips

Things I wish someone had told me before my first trip to Lisbon.

1
Use a credit union ATM, not a currency exchange. You will get a much better rate. Withdraw euros from a Multibanco ATM in the city center.
2
Capital One Venture X gives you free international data. Activate the travel feature before you fly. No SIM card needed.
3
Take Tram 28 before 9am. It runs through Alfama and Chiado. Scenic and free with your transit card. Crowded after 10am.
4
Bolt is cheaper than Uber in Lisbon. Both apps work well. Book a private transfer for airport runs to get a fixed price.
5
Marriott Bonvoy members get early check-in when available. Call ahead the day before to request it.
6
Pasteis de Belem is worth the line. Go early morning. Order a dozen to go. They taste different warm, straight from the oven.

Points & Miles

How to Book Your Flight with Points

Step-by-step: Amex Membership Rewards → Air Canada Aeroplan → TAP Air Portugal.

1
Log into your Amex account
Go to americanexpress.com, navigate to Membership Rewards, and click Transfer Points.
2
Select Air Canada Aeroplan
Transfer ratio is 1:1. Transfer 70,000 Amex points to get 70,000 Aeroplan miles. Transfers are instant.
3
Search TAP Air Portugal on Aeroplan
Go to aeroplan.com, search JFK to LIS. Business class costs 70,000 miles + $280 in taxes.
4
Complete the booking
Select your flight, enter passenger details, and pay the taxes with your card. Your miles cover the fare.
